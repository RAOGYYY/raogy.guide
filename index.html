About

I build software that solves real, on-ground business problems.

I'm Anwar. My focus is on building software for businesses that run on the street, not just on a screen — local shops, field service teams, and small business owners who need tools that actually work in the field, not just in a demo.

Paani Boss came out of a simple observation — RO water purifier service businesses in India were still running on notebooks, phone calls, and guesswork. So I built an end-to-end platform that gives shop owners a real dashboard and gives their technicians a real tool: live job assignment, GPS-verified site visits, TDS-based service prediction, digital billing, and route optimization, all in one app.

Paani Boss is live and working today — built for shop owners who manage the business and technicians who run the field, with everything tied together through one shared system.
